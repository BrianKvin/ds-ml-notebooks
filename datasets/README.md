### Global Air Quality Analytics

Interactive dashboard for global pollution tracking and environmental trend analysis.

**Focus areas**
- Pollutant distribution by region (`PM2.5`, `PM10`, `NO₂`, `CO`, `O₃`)
- Time-based trend analysis and regional comparison
- Identification of high-risk geographies for policy and planning

**Tools**
- Power BI
- SQL preprocessing
- Python/Pandas for ETL

**Source file**
- `AIr Quality.pbix`
